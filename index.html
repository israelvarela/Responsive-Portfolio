Who is Israel Varela?

Israel Varela is a seasoned professional who has tackled many role in his life. He has 15 years of experience creating digital marketing strategies for companies in the B2C, B2B, and eCommerce space. The company sizes he has worked with have been large International, National, and SMB's spanning a number of different verticals from Aviation, Real Estate, Healthcare, IT, Entertainment, Retail, and more.


In addition, Israel Varela has 10 yeas of experience as a Realtor helping his clients buy, sell, and lease residential real estate for their personal uses. He has also worked with a number of real estate investors for investment acquisitions, and sales to meet their investment portfolio goals

He has also achieved regional success as an Actor having been cast in local commercials, industrials, independent short and feature films, as well syndicated television shows such as Salem, The Leftovers, the Reelz Channel's Murder Made Me Famous, and The Price of Fame.
